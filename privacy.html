Who we are

Intelligence AI ("we," "us") operates getintelligence.co and provides AI lead capture and automation services for local service businesses. Contact: hello@getintelligence.co · 279-235-7067.

What we collect

When you visit our site or use our chat widget, we may collect:

  • Name, email, phone number, and messages you submit through the chat widget

We use this information to respond to inquiries, schedule demos, deliver our services, and improve our website. We do not sell your personal information.

We use subprocessors to operate our site:

These providers process data under their own privacy policies. We configure them only for business operations.

Our site and embedded widgets may use cookies or local storage for session functionality and chat continuity. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

SMS

SMS consent is collected only through our Lead Connector chat widget. Reply STOP to opt out of texts. Message and data rates may apply.

Mobile information sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services is permitted. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Retention

We retain contact and chat records as long as needed to respond to inquiries, deliver services, or meet legal obligations.

Your rights

California residents may request access to or deletion of personal information by emailing hello@getintelligence.co. We will respond within applicable legal timeframes.

Children

Our services are not directed at anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes
